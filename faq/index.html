Get Started

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy. Follow the instructions here. Basically, you upload a data dump, we process it and you download the recommendation results. You can use the recommendation results with anything you want. E.g. Give it to your sales agents or integrate it with your e-commerce platform.

I need something unique, can you help?

Yes. Rommy is a flexible recommendation engine with a core focus of only providing recommendations. This flexibility allows Rommy to be integrated and customised to anything you want. We can build any application/workflow for you with Rommy running at its core.

Do you need Personally Identifiable Information (PII) for this to work?

No. We prefer it if you don’t give us any PII of your customers. You will be informed if we find any PII in your data.

Where do I upload the file to? Is it secure?

Currently, all files that we receive from you will be stored securely and privately with our cloud provider. Only you and Rommy will have access to this data.

Discounts for students and Non Profit Organizations?

Yes, in the future. We are running a public beta which means you can request access and usage for free. Discounts will be available for Non Profit Organizations when our public beta ends.
