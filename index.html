Let us solve your customer recommendation challenges

Almost all companies with multiple products would like to sell additional products to existing customers. This is known as cross-selling. However, if you have a customer base of 10 million customers, it is unlikely that you would like to call each customer about every product. This would be very expensive and not very productive. In general, campaigns such as these get very low take-up rates with some campaigns requiring you to call up to 100 customers to be able to get a single sale. There must be a better way, right?
